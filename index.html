Here are my 5 Milestones of a Cell Phone

  • Rotary Dial Phone

    • The Rotary Dial Phone was first introduced to the world in 1889 by inventor Almon B. Strowger. On a rotary phone the digits are organized in a circular motion going counterclockwise, on top of the digits is a circular wheel which allows you to rotate the wheel with your finger.
